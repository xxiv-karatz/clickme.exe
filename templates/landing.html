FAQ

Common questions

Is my data stored?
No. All data is ephemeral and session-only. Nothing is written to a database. The moment you close or refresh your browser, your session and all analyzed messages are permanently gone. We are architecturally incapable of storing your data.
Does it require an account or registration?
No account, no email, no sign-up. Just open the app and start analyzing. Sessions are identified by a random UUID generated at runtime that disappears when you leave.
What AI model powers the analysis?
clickme.exe uses DeepSeek AI — a powerful open-weight model — with a specialized cybersecurity forensics system prompt engineered for behavioral analysis and MITRE ATT&CK mapping.
Can I analyze batch messages?
Yes. Upload a CSV file with a "message" column and the platform will analyze up to 20 messages in sequence, then aggregate the results into session-level analytics and a downloadable report.
Is this legal and ethical to use?
clickme.exe is a defensive tool built exclusively for analyzing received suspicious messages — not for generating phishing content. It is intended for security professionals, IT teams, educators, and individuals who want to understand attacks they've received. Do not use it to analyze messages you do not have permission to analyze.
